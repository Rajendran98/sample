Surveillance to a large extent today is being handled through a network of CCTV cameras with limited or no manpower to monitor the live feed. Due to this, a typical CCTV surveillance setup is used as a post-mortem tool to investigate security violations in the area of coverage.

Even in facilities where human monitoring is available, they are highly ineffective due to the inherent inadequacy of human beings to monitor multiple video feeds continuously.

Real-time Monitoring:

By analyzing live footage from the CCTV cameras in near-real-time, the platform obviates the need to setup a huge Command and Control Centre and deploy human resources for monitoring.

Color-coding:

Incidents are color coded into RED, YELLOW, GREEN, BLUE and GRAY based on severity to draw attention to the most relevant incidents so response can be provided efficiently.

Rule based alerts:

On detection of anomalies, the platform alerts relevant stakeholders and respondents based on pre-configured rules. Some of the anomalies captured by the platform are:

  • Unauthorized entry and exit of people and vehicles at secure locations.

  • Presence and Absence of personnel in defined stations.

  • Emergencies like fire, blast and accidents.

  • Over speeding of vehicles.

  • Activities like running, collapsing, crowding, climbing and scuffle.

  • Destructive acts like Vandalism and littering.

  • Physical defects in manufactured products.

  • Deviations in process followed in assembly lines.

Automatic Response:

When incidents of significance occur, the platform can provide real-time response on certain scenarios. Some of the typical responses possible are:

  • Operating a PTZ camera to zoom in on the area of incident.

  • Turning on Focus lights to shine on the area of incident.

  • Sounding a Siren.

  • Making Voice calls to a list of people.

  • Operating a Boom Barrier/Tyre Rippers/ Bollards.

  • Deploying a drone for closer scrutiny.

Response tracking:

The platform can track the action taken by the designated responders to identified incidents. If time-bound response is not provided it can also escalate by sending alerts to higher authorities in a pre-configured manner.

Post-facto Analysis:

Insights generated by analysing the live video footage are stored and presented through interactive dashboards helping users get deep insights on incidents without having to scan through hours of video footage.

Interactive Dashboard:


The platform offers an interactive dashboard to help stakeholders consume information of relevance without wasting too much time. The dashboard is highly customizable and can be modified to suit the needs of the end user.

Daily Capsule:

The platform provides daily capsule for users to get deep insights on the area under surveillance through visual tools.
