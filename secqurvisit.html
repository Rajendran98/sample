SECQURVISIT

The era of digital and social media has created newer challenges for organizations to work around to safeguard their interests. People and vehicles entering the premises of an organization are both a potential threat and an opportunity. It is essential for organizations to handle entry and exit of people and vehicles efficiently by leveraging technology.

Use of the right technologies can ensure people and vehicles enter only with appropriate authorizations, don’t stray and are easily tracked while enhancing the experience at the first touch point.

The conventional method of security guard entering information on a register to keep record of people entering and exiting is highly inefficient and doesn’t even ensure availability of accurate information when needed.

Apart from being inefficient, the human element impacts the visitors’ experience leaving a not so positive experience at the first point of contactm.

SecqurVisit:

SecqurVisit offers an efficient and extremely user friendly system that can aid the organization in safeguarding against transgressions by visitors while offering a superlative experience as they enter and exit facilities.

This Visitor Management System (VMS) is also integrated with SecqurIN – and AI powered attendance management system and SecqurAsset – an asset management system giving a unified view of inflow and outflow to any facility.


SecqurVisit:

This Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered solution offers a host of features to enable faster admission process, reducing the time spent by visitors at the entry. Thanks to the efficient and user friendly process, the security guard at the gate can handle visitor authorization eliminating the need for dedicated manpower at reception.

The system works with multiple access control mechanisms including Face Recognition, Biometric Reader, RFID and QR code to scrutinize visitors as they attempt to enter the premises eliminating manual errors and impersonation.

Type Supported

  • Visitors with prior invitation from hosts.
  • Cold calling visitors without prior invitations.
  • Delivery/Dispatch vehicles entering for handling material.
  • Contract/Temporary employees who visit for short/long durations.
  • Employees from other branches visiting for short durations.
  • Contractors visiting for job works.
